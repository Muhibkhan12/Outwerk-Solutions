Our stack‑agnostic probes integrate with cloud, on‑prem, and hybrid environments. You get a unified view of health metrics, logs, and traces, so your team can focus on optimisation instead of firefighting.

What we monitor

  • Server health (CPU, memory, disk, processes)

  • Cloud services (AWS, Azure, GCP) metrics
  • SSL expiry, certificate validity
  • Power & environmental sensors (datacenter)
